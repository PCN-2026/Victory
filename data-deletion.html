Data Deletion Instructions

Victory App stores only your email address and chanting session records.

How to delete your data:

  1. Open the Victory App
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap Reset all data
  4. Confirm the reset

This permanently deletes all your chanting history, streak, and Daimoku Chart progress from our database.

To also delete your account, email us at foongcl1@gmail.com and we will remove your account within 7 days.
